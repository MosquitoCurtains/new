## Page Content

### Introduction

Welcome to Mosquito Curtains. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website (www.mosquitocurtains.com) and use our services.

By using our website and services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

#### 1.1 Personal Information

When you interact with Mosquito Curtains, we may collect personal information such as:

- Name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Mailing address
- Payment details (processed securely via third-party payment processors)
- Account login credentials (if applicable)

#### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information through cookies and analytics tools, including:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Pages visited and time spent on the site
- Referring website or source

#### 1.3 SMS & Communication Data

If you opt in to receive SMS communications, we collect and store:

- Your phone number
- Consent status
- Message history and engagement

---

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

- To fulfill orders and provide services – processing transactions, shipping, and order confirmations.
- To communicate with you – sending order updates, customer service responses, and requested information.
- For SMS marketing and promotions – only with your explicit consent.
- To improve our website and user experience – using analytics and cookies to optimize performance.
- For legal and security reasons – to prevent fraud, comply with laws, and enforce our policies.

---

### 3. SMS Communication & Consent

#### 3.1 How You Opt In

You may opt-in to receive SMS messages from Mosquito Curtains when you:

- Check the consent box on our website lead forms.
- Submit your phone number via Facebook Lead Forms.
- Text START to subscribe.

#### 3.2 Message Frequency & Data Charges

- We send SMS messages related to orders, promotions, and product updates.
- Message frequency varies, and standard message and data rates may apply based on your carrier's terms.

#### 3.3 How to Opt Out

- You can unsubscribe at any time by replying STOP to any text message.
- For assistance, reply HELP or contact our support team.

---

### 4. Sharing & Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

- Service providers & payment processors – to fulfill orders, process payments, and deliver communications.
- Third-party marketing partners – only with your consent.
- Legal authorities – if required by law or to prevent fraud or abuse.

---

### 5. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement the following measures:

- Encryption – Secure SSL encryption for transactions.
- Access controls – Restricted access to sensitive data.
- Regular audits – Monitoring for potential security risks.

---

### 6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

#### 6.1 GDPR (For EU Users)

- Right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.
- Right to object to processing and withdraw consent.

#### 6.2 CCPA (For California Residents)

- Right to know what personal data we collect.
- Right to request deletion of your personal data.
- Right to opt out of data sales (we do not sell your data).

To exercise your rights, email us at help@mosquitocurtains.com.

---

### 7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

- Analyze website traffic and performance.
- Remember user preferences for a personalized experience.

You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings.

---

### 8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies separately.

---

### 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date.

---

For questions or privacy-related concerns, contact us at:
